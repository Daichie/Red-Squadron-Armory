## Attachment detail formatting

Weapon attachment details are now rendered as organized readable specs instead of one long paragraph.

Example:

```text
Stock → MP5A3 Fixed Collapsible
Magazine → MP5 9x19mm Parabellum 30rd Magazine
Optic → HWS XPS3
Barrel → Navy 8.9 with Tri-Lug OD Suppressor
```
